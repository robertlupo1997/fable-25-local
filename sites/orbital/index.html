The Cooperative

Perigee Commons is a non-profit orbital cooperative that pools satellite resources across four partner research institutions — providing equitable access to Earth observation data without proprietary lock-in. Our twelve-vehicle constellation delivers global coverage every 90 minutes, shared under a Creative Commons Attribution license.

NAL

Nordic Atmosphere Lab

Arctic sea ice extent, atmospheric column NO₂, and polar stratospheric cloud dynamics. Oslo, Norway.

PBCO

Pacific Basin Climate Observatory

Sea surface temperature, ENSO precursors, and typhoon track validation. Yokohama, Japan.

ARSI

Alpine Remote Sensing Institute

Glacier mass balance, alpine land cover change, and European particulate matter monitoring. Zurich, Switzerland.

SRS

Subarctic Research Station

Permafrost thaw dynamics, boreal forest carbon flux, and subarctic AIS vessel traffic. Yellowknife, Canada.
