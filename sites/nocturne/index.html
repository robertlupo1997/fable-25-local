About This Guide

The six species documented here were collected over four decades of fieldwork in the highland birch and beech forests of a range that is difficult to reach and easily forgotten. None appear in any regional fauna or catalogue that can be readily found.

Plates were engraved by an unnamed studio working from water-damaged specimens. The accuracy of the illustrations is therefore approximate; the taxonomy is genuine.

Light the lamp in the scene above to observe flight behaviour. Moth attraction radius varies by species and lunar phase — the moon in the upper right reflects the actual phase at the time of your visit.
