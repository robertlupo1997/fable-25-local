The Eight Principles of 永

The single character 永 (yǒng, "forever") contains the eight essential brushstrokes of regular-script calligraphy. Every other character is built from these foundations.

侧 Cè Dot
勒 Lè Horizontal
努 Nǔ Vertical
趯 Tì Hook

掠 Lüè Long Sweep
啄 Zhuó Peck
磔 Zhé Right Sweep
永

Calligraphy is not writing with a brush — it is listening through one. Speed becomes width. Hesitation becomes weight. The ink does not lie: what you feel in your hand appears on the paper, and no stroke can be recalled.

inkwell is a space to begin. The character 永 guides your hand through the eight principles that compose every character in regular script. Set the guide aside when you are ready, and write freely. The seal at the end is yours to place.
